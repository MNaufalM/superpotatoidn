About Us

We Are The Biggest Supplier Of Fresh Potatoes

Why Choose Us ?

We grow potatoes in mountainous areas with an average temperature of 18 degrees Celsius. As a major supplier, we are the largest in East Java, with a total land area of around 3600 hectares and a productivity of 25 tons per hectare. Our contribution has covered about 60% of the total potato demand in East Java Province, Indonesia, which has a population of about 39.74 million. Our potatoes recognized by most of Indonesians cause our product all fresh and have fluffy taste.
